## About

This is my **personal portfolio website**, designed to showcase my skills, experience, and projects.  
It provides a modern and interactive way to introduce myself to potential employers, clients, and fellow developers.

### Development & Responsibilities

- **Elegant UI/UX** – A sleek, modern, and responsive design.  
- **Dark Mode** – Smooth dark theme for better readability.  
- **About Me Section** – A brief introduction and my tech stack.  
- **Blog** – Sharing insights, solutions, and experiences.  
- **Projects Showcase** – A collection of my best work, including GitHub links.  
- **Navigation Bar** – Easy access to different sections.  
- **Social Links** – Connect with me on **LinkedIn**, **GitHub**, and more.  

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
- **Next.js** – For a fast and SEO-friendly React framework.  
- **Tailwind CSS** – For styling and responsive design.  
- **TypeScript** – Ensuring type safety and maintainability.  
- **Framer Motion** – Smooth animations and transitions.
