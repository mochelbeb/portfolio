## About

Money Manager is a financial dashboard application designed to help users track expenses, manage budgets, and gain insights into their financial health with an intuitive and modern interface.

### Development & Responsibilities

As a Full-Stack Developer, I have been responsible for designing and developing the application, ensuring seamless integration of the frontend and backend. My responsibilities include:

- **Secure Authentication & Role-Based Access Control** using Supabase Auth.  
- **Expense & Budget Tracking** with detailed analytics and visual reports.  
- **Bank & Payment Integration** supporting **VISA, Wise, PayPal**.  
- **Interactive Dashboard** with charts and statistics for better financial insights.  
- **Multi-Currency Support** and customizable categories for transactions.  
- **State Management** optimized with Zustand for smooth performance.  
- **CI/CD Pipelines** ensuring efficient deployments and updates.
